Associate BPM Consultant

As a Consultant at Counterpoint, you will:

  • Work with customers to identify and model business processes using commercial and OSS BPM software
  • Work directly with our customers to translate business needs into technical solutions
  • Design, develop and test software
  • Work independently or cooperatively within software development teams
  • Provide follow up support and training to our customers
  • Participate in internal software development projects

Location: Vienna, VA

Travel: Must be willing to travel to customer sites (generally in the D.C. metro area)

Desired Skills and Experience

Required Qualifications

We are looking for intelligent, responsible and committed individuals who possess:

  • BA/BS from an accredited university
  • Demonstrated record of excellence inside and outside of the classroom
  • Software development experience or interest in pursuing a career in technology

Preferred Qualifications

  • BA/BS specifically in computer science or engineering
  • Knowledge of Web Development Technologies and Libraries (HTML, JS, CSS, jQuery)
  • Knowledge of Relational Databases & SQL
  • Software development experience (Java, Java Web Frameworks and / or .NET)
  • BPM development experience with Appian, Cordys, Metastorm, Micropact, Pegasystems, Opentext, Fuijitsu or other commercial or open source BPMS
